Service process

Step 1

Consulting on expressions and aquarium methods

Survey the actual construction site, discuss with customers to clearly understand their wishes and preferences. Based on that, we will advise on the most suitable pool style and size.

Step 2

Prepare quotes and design tasks

Depending on the size, level and style of the lake, SGL Vietnam will consider and quote customers the most specific and accurate price.

Step 3

Prepare conceptual design documents

Once you have clearly understood the customer's wishes as well as the details about the shape and style of the koi fish pond, after signing the contract, the company's team of architects will prepare a conceptual design document to send. to customers.

Step 4

Prepare basic design and construction design documents

After sending the preliminary design drawing (concept), we will continue to discuss to find out the level of customer satisfaction with the drawing. After that, the drawing will continue to be perfected to create the most perfect product.

Step 5

Construction

Engineers and construction staff will construct the project according to the developed documents. The project supervision engineer will ensure construction progress and other related factors when constructing a koi pond.
